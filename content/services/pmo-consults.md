# When existing PMOs need a tune-up

Even mature PMOs hit friction. Prioritization debates drag on. Capacity planning drifts from reality. Reports don't show what leaders need. Our consulting engagements diagnose what's slowing your team down and deliver targeted improvements.

## Common engagements

- **Intake & prioritization reviews** — Streamline how work gets proposed, scored, and approved.
- **Capacity management** — Bring predictability to resource planning and allocation.
- **Delivery process optimization** — Reduce cycle time and rework across your program lifecycle.
- **Reporting redesign** — Build reports that actually drive decisions.
- **Tooling assessments** — Evaluate whether your current tools support your delivery model.

## How it works

Most PMO Consults engagements run 4-8 weeks. We start with a short diagnostic, produce a clear set of recommendations, and work with your team to implement the changes that matter most. No long-term retainers — we're in, we help, we move on.
