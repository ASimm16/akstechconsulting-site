# When the stakes are high

Some initiatives are too important to hand to an inexperienced PM, and too complex to run off the side of someone's desk. These are the engagements where we step in as the senior leader — owning the outcome, coordinating stakeholders, managing risk, and driving delivery.

## Where we help

- **Platform migrations & technology transformations** — Complex multi-team programs with tight interdependencies.
- **New product launches** — From concept validation through go-to-market.
- **Organizational change initiatives** — Programs that touch multiple functions and require executive coordination.
- **Turnaround programs** — Rescue work on initiatives that have stalled or lost direction.

## How we lead

We embed with your team as the program owner for the duration of the initiative — typically 6-18 months. We don't just report on status; we drive decisions, manage stakeholders, remove blockers, and ensure the program lands. On the way out, we transition ownership cleanly to your internal team.
