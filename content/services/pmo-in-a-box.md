# How we build your PMO

Most growing organizations know they need a PMO but can't spare the months required to design one from scratch. PMO in a Box solves that. We bring a proven framework, adapt it to your operating model, and have your PMO running within 90 days.

## What's included

The PMO in a Box engagement delivers five foundational capabilities:

- **Planning** — A repeatable intake and prioritization process that aligns work to strategic direction and optimizes capacity utilization.
- **Execution** — Standardized delivery lifecycle, roles & responsibilities, and cross-team dependency management.
- **Process** — Documented procedures for prioritization, resource estimation, capacity management, and portfolio management.
- **Tools** — Recommended tooling for project and program management, software delivery, and reporting.
- **Reporting** — Portfolio priority list, portfolio status reports, and team allocation reports that give leaders visibility into delivery.

## The 90-day roadmap

We launch your PMO through a phased approach: assess and design in the first 30 days, implement and train in the next 30, and stabilize and hand off in the final 30. By day 90, your PMO is operational and producing the reports leadership needs to make decisions.
